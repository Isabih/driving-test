# Camera Placement by Stage

The field design follows the uploaded exam PDF.

## Stage 1 — Figure 8
Use 2 cameras.

- Cam S1 overhead:
  - mounted on a central pole or structure
  - sees the full 8-course and direction flow
  - best for wrong direction and line touch
- Cam S1 side:
  - diagonal entry/exit view
  - best for foot-down and rider behavior

## Stage 2 — Zigzag
Use 1 elevated diagonal camera.

- Mount at side midpoint
- Cover the full slalom if possible with zoom/lens adjustment

## Stage 3 — Narrow
Use 1 overhead or high straight camera.

- Must clearly see lane entry and exit
- Important for measuring time spent inside the narrow lane

## Stage 4 — Emergency Stop
Use 1 long side camera.

- Must cover approach, braking zone, and stop zone
- If you later expand, this is the first stage worth adding a second camera to

## Minimum camera set

- Stage 1: 2 cameras
- Stage 2: 1 camera
- Stage 3: 1 camera
- Stage 4: 1 camera

Total minimum: 5 cameras
